Infant Vocalizations Task

Infants make a wide variety of sounds - some are speech-like and others are not. The purpose of this task is to decide if an infant vocalization is speech-like or not. You will listent to 20 infant vocalizations and determine if they are speech-like or not.

    Instructions:

  • Click the arrow to play the infant vocalization (up to three times)
  • Decide whether the infant vocalization is speech-like or not
  • Click the button with your choice to go to the next vocalization
